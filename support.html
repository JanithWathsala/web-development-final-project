Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a space flight?

To book a space flight, simply navigate to our homepage and use our booking system. Select your desired flight date, choose your spacecraft, and follow the prompts to complete your reservation.

What are the requirements for space travel?

All passengers must be at least 18 years old and in good physical health. A medical clearance from an approved physician is required before your flight.

What is included in the ticket price?

Your ticket includes the space flight, necessary training, spacesuit rental, and in-flight refreshments. Accommodation before and after the flight is not included.

Booking Policies

Please review our booking policies before making a reservation:

  • Full payment is required at the time of booking
  • Cancellations made 30 days or more before the flight date are eligible for a full refund
  • Cancellations made within 30 days of the flight date are non-refundable
  • Flight dates may be changed subject to availability and a rebooking fee

Preparing for Your Flight

Once your booking is confirmed, you will receive detailed information about:

  • Pre-flight training schedule
  • Required medical examinations
  • What to bring (and what not to bring) on your space journey
  • Check-in procedures and launch day timeline
