PRIVACY POLICY


Effective Date: 15 February 2023

Welcome to Astral Flights. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled safely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.


1.Information Collection

Personal Information
We may collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment details
  • Address

Non-Personal Information We may also collect non-personal information such as: Browser type IP address Browsing behavior Device information Referring site Collection Methods Forms: Information you provide directly through forms on our website. Cookies: Data collected automatically through cookies to enhance user experience. Third-Party Services: Information gathered through third-party analytics and payment processing services.


2.Information Usage

Purpose of Use To process and confirm your bookings. To communicate with you about your reservations and our services. To improve our website and services through analytics. To send you promotional offers and newsletters, if you opt-in.Sharing Information We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. 3.Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption
  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology
  • Regular security audits

Opt-Out Options If you wish to unsubscribe from our newsletters or promotional emails, you can opt out by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of these emails or contacting us directly.

Cookies Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site usage, and assist in our marketing efforts. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level. Third-Party Tracking We may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to track and analyze non-personal usage information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
